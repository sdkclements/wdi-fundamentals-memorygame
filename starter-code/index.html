Instructions:

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexexso, or Paris, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.



This game is best! It keeps you sharp and its quick and fun!
